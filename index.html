Door Supervisor - SIA Level 2 Qualification

Become a licensed Door Supervisor with our comprehensive training program. This course meets all SIA requirements for obtaining a Door Supervisor license in the UK.

Course Duration

6 days of intensive training

Certification

Nationally recognized Level 2 qualification

Course Overview

Our Door Supervisor training program is designed to provide you with all the skills and knowledge required to work as a licensed Door Supervisor in the UK. The course covers conflict management, physical intervention, emergency procedures, and legal aspects of the role.

What You'll Learn

  • Principles of working in the private security industry
  • Application of conflict management in the private security industry
  • Physical intervention skills and techniques
  • Emergency procedures and first aid awareness
  • Legal aspects relevant to door supervision
  • Communication skills and customer service

Course Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Valid ID (Passport or Driving License)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • Basic level of English understanding

Career Opportunities

After completing this course, you can work in various venues including:

  • Bars, pubs, and nightclubs
  • Music venues and festivals
  • Shopping centers and retail outlets
  • Corporate buildings and offices
  • Sports stadiums and events
